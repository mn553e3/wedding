Travel Information

Welcome to Miami! Here's some travel information to help you plan your trip for our special day.

Hotel Recommendations

  • The Miami Beach EDITION - A luxury hotel with oceanfront views and elegant rooms.
  • W South Beach - Stylish accommodations with vibrant nightlife options.
  • Mandarin Oriental, Miami - Five-star hotel offering a serene retreat with a private beach.
  • Fontainebleau Miami Beach - Iconic luxury hotel with a storied history and modern amenities.

Transportation Tips

  • Nearest Airport: Miami International Airport (MIA)
  • Getting Around: Consider renting a car for convenience, or use taxis and ride-sharing services like Uber and Lyft.
  • Public Transit: Miami's Metrorail, Metrobus, and the free Metromover are great options for getting around the city.
